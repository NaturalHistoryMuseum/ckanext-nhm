The Natural History Museum is committed to open access and open science, and has launched the Data Portal to make its research and collections datasets available online. It allows anyone to explore, download and reuse the data for their own research.

Museum collection

Our natural history collection is one of the most important in the world, documenting 4.5 billion years of life, the Earth and the solar system. Almost all animal, plant, mineral and fossil groups are represented.

More about our collections

Research datasets

The Museum has over 600 curators, researchers, scientific associates, students and volunteers. Our scientists generate over 1,000 scientific papers published annually and datasets from these publications will be available through the portal.

Help us improve our collection

Digitising and databasing our vast collections is a huge and evolving task. As a result some records on the Data Portal may contain erroneous data. If you do find a record that needs correcting, please tell us about it at data@nhm.ac.uk.

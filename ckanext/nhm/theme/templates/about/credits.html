    The Data Portal is a window onto the Museum’s research and collections data, and is the result of decades of work from the Museum's science staff.

    Open source

    The Data Portal is built with CKAN, an open-source data-publishing platform.

    The Museum has extended and customised CKAN, and this code is licensed under the terms of the GNU GPL v3.0, and available from the Natural History Museum’s code repository.

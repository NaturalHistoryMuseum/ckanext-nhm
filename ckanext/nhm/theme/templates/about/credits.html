    The data portal has been developed by the Natural History Museum’s Biodiversity Informatics Group, with the help and support of teams throughout the Museum.

    1. Vince Smith - SRO
    2. Ben Scott - Lead architect
    3. Alice Heaton - Senior developer

    As a window onto the Museum’s data the success of the portal is a credit to the scientists producing the data and curating the collections.

    Open source

    The data portal is built with CKAN, an open-source data-publishing platform.

    The Museum has extended and customised CKAN, and this code is licensed under the terms of the GNU GPL v3.0 [TBC], and available from The Natural History Museum’s Github account.
